## Become a Member

Joining SIX03 Run Club is easy and affordable! We welcome runners of all abilities, from beginners to experienced marathoners.

### Membership Benefits

✅ **Group Runs** - Access to all weekly group runs and training sessions  
✅ **Coaching** - Guidance from experienced runners and certified coaches  
✅ **Race Discounts** - Discounted entry fees for club-organized races  
✅ **Social Events** - Invitations to pub runs, parties, and celebrations  
✅ **Training Plans** - Free access to structured training programs  
✅ **Club Gear** - Exclusive club apparel and merchandise discounts  
✅ **Partner Discounts** - Special offers from local running stores and pubs  
✅ **Online Community** - Access to our private Facebook group and Strava club  

### Membership Tiers

#### Individual Membership - $40/year
Perfect for solo runners who want to join the community.

#### Family Membership - $60/year
Great value for households with multiple runners (up to 4 family members).

#### Student Membership - $25/year
Discounted rate for full-time students (valid student ID required).

#### Trial Membership - FREE
Try us out! Attend 3 group runs for free before committing to membership.

### How to Join

1. **Try Us Out** - Come to any group run as a guest (first 3 runs are free!)
2. **Sign Up** - Fill out our [membership form](#) (coming soon)
3. **Pay Dues** - Submit payment via Venmo, PayPal, or check
4. **Get Your Gear** - Order your club singlet or tech tee
5. **Start Running!** - You're officially part of the SIX03 family

### Membership Year

Our membership year runs from January 1st to December 31st. Join anytime - dues are prorated for new members joining mid-year.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need to be fast to join?**  
Absolutely not! We have runners of all paces, from 6:00/mile to 12:00/mile and beyond. Everyone is welcome.

**What if I'm a beginner?**  
Perfect! We have beginner-friendly groups and experienced members who love helping new runners.

**Can I come to events if I'm not a member?**  
Yes! Guests are welcome to try out 3 group runs for free. After that, we ask that you become a member.

**Are there any age requirements?**  
Members must be 16 or older. Runners aged 16-18 need parental consent and must be accompanied by a parent/guardian.

**Can I cancel my membership?**  
We don't offer refunds, but memberships are inexpensive and you can choose not to renew the following year.

**What if I move away or get injured?**  
We understand life happens! While we can't offer refunds, you're welcome to rejoin when you're ready.
